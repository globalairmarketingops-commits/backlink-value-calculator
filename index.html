Guardrails

  • Relevance beats authority. A DR-40 aviation site is worth more to us than a DR-70 generic-business one. Don’t let a big number seduce the deal.
  • Reject toxic links. Link farms, PBNs, “SEO directories” with thousands of unrelated outbound links, or deindexed sites = zero or negative value. Decline.
  • We negotiate from strength. A dofollow from GlobalAir.com is a premium asset (30 years, 200K+ ranking keywords). Never swap it 1:1 to a small site — push for one-way, or extract extra value.
  • Nofollow ≠ worthless, but ≠ SEO. Its value is referral traffic + brand, not ranking equity. Price it low.
